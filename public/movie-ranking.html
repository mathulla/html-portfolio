The best movies according to Ann

My top 3 movies of all time

The Incredibles

An animated tale about a superhero family. Great for all ages.

The Help

Set in the backdrop of Mississpi in the 1960's, two women work up the courage to co-author a book about their lives as coloured maids.

The Big Short

A funny yet educational film that explains how the 2008 financial crisis unfolded, and the few individuals who saw it coming.
